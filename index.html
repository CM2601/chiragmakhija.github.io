Hospitality Insights Power BI Dashboard

Introducing the Hospitality Management Power BI Dashboard, meticulously crafted to provide comprehensive insights into key metrics for the hospitality industry.

  • Utilizing Week-on-Week (WoW) analysis, the dashboard tracks changes in various performance indicators, including Revenue, Occupancy, ADR (Average Daily Rate), RevPAR (Revenue Per Available Room), Realization, and DSRN (Daily Sellable Room Nights).
  • The dashboard incorporates complex calculations to derive WoW change percentages for each metric, enabling stakeholders to monitor trends and identify areas for improvement effectively.
  • With detailed breakdowns of formulae and intuitive visualizations, this dashboard empowers hospitality managers to make data-driven decisions and optimize business performance.
